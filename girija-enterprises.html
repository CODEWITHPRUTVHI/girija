About Girija Enterprises

Building a sustainable future through responsible plastic trading

Our Mission

At Girija Enterprises, we are committed to creating a circular economy for plastic materials. We connect raw plastic suppliers with recycling facilities and manufacturers, ensuring that valuable plastic waste is transformed into reusable resources.

Our Experience

With over a decade of experience in the plastic trading industry, we have built strong relationships with suppliers, recyclers, and manufacturers across India. Our network spans major industrial hubs, enabling efficient material flow and competitive pricing.

Quality Assurance

Every material passing through Girija Enterprises undergoes rigorous quality verification. We ensure transparency in grading, fair pricing, and reliable supply chains for all our partners.

Sustainability Focus

We believe in responsible business practices that benefit both the environment and the economy. By facilitating plastic recycling, we contribute to reducing landfill waste and promoting sustainable manufacturing.
